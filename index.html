एक कदम स्वच्छता की ओर

We are initiating a Social Media Campaign, from 21st Jan to 31st Jan, 2020 with its roots from BITS Vizag regarding Swachh Survekshan Cleanliness Event to promote our Vizag City as well as educate the Young Adults especially regarding the event and the feedback to be given. As a part of this, we are arranging a Awareness Program cum Citizen Feedback Entry and a Social Media Contest across F.I.T (Facebook-Instagram-Twitter). Coming to the Contest, its simply a selfie based activity which will allow students to take a selfie before the banner along with the Placards containing the Mascot of #SS and upload them with #hashtags of Swachh Survekshan and our Associate Partners. The Entries with the highest number of likes by 31st Jan, 2020 12.00 pm will be declared as the Winner and Runner and awarded Certificates on behalf of the Campaign and exciting cash prizes as well as discounts too

SWACHH SURVEKSHAN 2020

The Union Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs releases a list of the cleanest cities in India after undertaking a comprehensive four-step survey. One part of the survey is to gather the citizens’ feedback on their local municipality’s work through the year. The GVMC therefore has amped up its game ahead of the survey. It has invited Vizagites to register their votes by dialing toll-free number ‘1969’ or via the Swacchta web application. As per a recent update, Visakhapatnam has topped the charts in the category of citizen feedback
